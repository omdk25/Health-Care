Bowie Blade Readers Choice Award.

For the second consecutive year, we've been recognized for a Bowie Blade Readers Choice Award. In 2019, we were chosen: Best Hospital, Best Occupational Therapy, Best Physical Therapy and Best Sleep Specialist categories. This award is part of an annual tradition which allows customers to vote on businesses for outstanding products and services in the community.

2020 Wellness at Work Awards – a statewide honor.

Doctors Community Hospital has been recognized with a gold-level rating in the 2020 Wellness at Work Awards – a statewide honor. The rating recognizes exemplary accomplishments in the promotion of workplace health. The Wellness at Work Awards are granted by the Healthiest Maryland Businesses program, which was founded in 2010 by the Maryland Department of Health and Mental Hygiene.

Cardiac Rehabilitation Program

Our Cardiac Rehabilitation Program has earned certification by the American Association of Cardiovascular and Pulmonary Rehabilitation (AACVPR). It is the only peer-reviewed accreditation that recognizes cardiovascular and pulmonary rehabilitation leaders who offer enhanced standards of care. This prestigious recognition further demonstrates our dedication to providing high-quality medical care to the community.
